CfP: CPSS 2021

Nils Ole Tippenhauer
Nov 17, 2020 1 min read service

I am co-chairing the 7th ACM Cyber-Physical System Security Workshop (CPSS 2021) next year, together with Mauro Conti. CPSS is a long established workshop for research on security of Cyber-Physical Systems (such as medical devices, manufacturing and industrial control, robotics and autonomous vehicles). The workshop will run for the seventh time, co-located with ASIACCS (7 June ‘21, Hong Kong).

The submission deadline Januar 27 AoE. Direct Submission Link

More details on the website and the full CfP.

Submitted papers can be up to 12 pages including appendices and references. Submissions must use the ACM SIG Proceedings Templates.

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: Jan 27, 2021 (23:59 Anywhere on Earth time)
  • Notification of Acceptance/Rejection: Feb 26, 2021
  • Camera Ready Papers Due: March 15, 2021 (hard deadline, see notification mails)
